DRY Blog

Sharing the Stories of Women in Tech

DRY blog is a platform for women in technology. It was created as a resource, outlet, and community for women in the tech space. There are too many stories that go untold about accomplishments, failures, and experiences in this growing field. Our hope in building this platform is that it will serve as a catalyst for action and a connector of experiences surrounding diversity in technology. I co-founded this project with a dear friend and fellow developer, Lucy Cifferello.
